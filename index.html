FAQ

Is the Proof Pack acceptable for auditors and customer reviews?

Yes — we follow common evidence conventions (timestamps, scope notes, policy versions) aligned to SOC 2 / ISO 27001 categories.

Redaction-first intake, least-privilege access, and watermarking on outbound artifacts you approve for sharing. No raw data required.

Do you support NIST/HIPAA or custom questionnaires?

Yes. Tell us your framework needs during intake; we’ll map artifacts accordingly.
