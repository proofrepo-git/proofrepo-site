How ProofRepo works

1) Pick a plan

Self-serve checkout (Stripe) or the request form below. No calls required.

2) Submit artifacts

Manual upload (default): share the same evidence you already prepare for audits (settings screenshots/exports, logs, policy excerpts). We organize and label it for you.

3) Delivery & refresh

Receive your Proof Pack in 24–72h. We’ll send refresh reminders on your cadence (monthly / quarterly / annual).

Local Collector (premium)

Optional time-saver: run a small tool locally to gather repo metadata & logs; you review & approve what to share. Nothing leaves your machine until you submit.

GitHub App (read-only, later)

Grant minimal scopes so we can auto-pull branch protections & CI status. You still review before sharing.

Privacy by design

Least-privilege intake, redaction guidance, and watermarks on artifacts approved for external sharing. No raw data required.
