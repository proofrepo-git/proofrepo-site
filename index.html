Evidence arrives organized by control area with a complete manifest — mapped to specific controls, ready to drop into your workpapers, reports, or compliance documentation.

Everything you need to collect evidence efficiently

Guided enough to eliminate back-and-forth. Flexible enough to fit how your firm actually works.

Curated evidence library

519 items across SOC 2, ISO 27001, SOC 1, HIPAA, NIST CSF, CMMC, and GDPR. Each with descriptions, expected formats, control references, and tips that reduce re-requests.

Customize per engagement

Edit descriptions, add custom items, remove what doesn't apply, set per-framework template levels. Every request reflects your standards, not a generic checklist.

Save any request as a reusable template. Next engagement, load it in one click and adjust. Build consistency across your practice without rebuilding from scratch.

Dashboard and auto follow-ups

Track every request in one place — who's submitted, who's overdue, what's coming due. Automated reminders go out before deadlines so you focus on review, not chasing.

Simple, transparent pricing

One credit per request. No subscriptions, no per-seat fees, no annual contracts.

Built for Sensitive Data

Your clients trust you with their evidence. Here's how we protect it.

AES-256 at Rest

Files encrypted in Cloudflare R2 using the same standard as banks and government systems.

TLS 1.2+ in Transit

Every connection is HTTPS-only. Data is encrypted between your browser and our servers.

90-Day Auto-Delete

Uploaded files are automatically purged via R2 lifecycle rules. No manual cleanup needed.

Cloudflare SOC 2

Runs on Cloudflare Workers, R2, and KV — all SOC 2 Type II and ISO 27001 certified.
