How ProofRepo works (today)

1) Share or open the link

An auditor or company goes to proofrepo.com. Auditors can share the link plus a short code so uploads are routed correctly.

2) Upload evidence

Attach the SOC 2 artifacts you already use: access reviews, change logs, vendor exports, and related files. ProofRepo stores them only long enough to generate the pack.

3) Receive a ProofPack

We generate a structured manifest and a human-friendly viewer that groups files and flags gaps (for example, missing vendor list). You receive a link via email.
