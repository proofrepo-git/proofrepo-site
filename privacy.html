Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 28, 2026

Privacy at a Glance

  • We collect only what's needed to provide the service
  • Files are automatically deleted after 90 days
  • We never sell your data or use it for advertising
  • We don't use tracking cookies or analytics
  • Your files are encrypted at rest and in transit
  • You can request deletion of your data at any time

1. What We Collect

We collect information necessary to provide the evidence collection service:

Data Type Purpose Retention
Email addresses Send links and notifications With request record (30 days)
Company names Identify submissions With request record (30 days)
Uploaded files Deliver to auditor 90 days (auto-deleted)
Payment info Process purchases (via Stripe) Handled by Stripe

What we don't collect: We don't use analytics, tracking pixels, or advertising cookies. We don't collect device fingerprints or build user profiles.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your information to:

  • Send evidence request links to clients
  • Notify auditors when evidence is submitted
  • Process credit purchases
  • Provide download access to uploaded files
  • Send service-related communications (no marketing)

3. Who We Share With

By design: When you use ProofRepo, evidence is shared between the auditor and client specified in each request. This is the core function of the service.

Service providers: We use trusted providers to operate the service:

  • Cloudflare — Hosting, storage, and security (US-based, SOC 2 certified)
  • Stripe — Payment processing (PCI DSS Level 1 certified)
  • Resend — Email delivery (US-based)

We never: Sell your data, share it with advertisers, or use it for purposes beyond providing the service.

4. Data Retention

Data Retention Period
Uploaded files 90 days (automatic deletion)
Request records 30 days
Credit/billing records 12 months

You can delete submissions immediately through the Pack Viewer without waiting for automatic deletion.

5. Security

We protect your data with:

  • AES-256 encryption at rest
  • TLS 1.2+ encryption in transit
  • Time-limited signed URLs (72-hour expiry)
  • No persistent user accounts (link-based access)

For more details, see our Security Practices page.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — Request a copy of your data
  • Deletion — Request we delete your data
  • Correction — Request we correct inaccurate data
  • Portability — Receive your data in a standard format

To exercise these rights, email privacy@proofrepo.com

7. Cookies

We use minimal, essential cookies only:

  • Session management (keeping you on the right page)
  • Security tokens (preventing abuse)

We don't use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or third-party analytics.

8. Children's Privacy

ProofRepo is a business service not intended for children. We don't knowingly collect data from anyone under 16. If you believe a child has provided us data, please contact us.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Material changes will be communicated via email or notice on our website. The "last updated" date at the top indicates when changes were made.

10. Contact

Privacy questions? Email us at privacy@proofrepo.com

General support: support@proofrepo.com
