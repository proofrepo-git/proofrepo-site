1 Introduction

ProofRepo ("we," "us," or "our") operates the ProofRepo compliance evidence collection platform at proofrepo.com (the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Service.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data transparently. We collect only the minimum data necessary to provide our service, and we delete it automatically after a defined retention period.

Our Privacy Commitment

We don't sell your data. We don't use tracking scripts. We don't build profiles on you. We collect only what's necessary to deliver evidence from clients to auditors, and we automatically delete everything after 90 days.

2 Data We Collect

We collect different types of data depending on how you interact with ProofRepo:

Information You Provide Directly

Data Type When Collected Purpose
Email addresses When creating an evidence request or purchasing credits To send magic links, receipts, and notifications
Company names When creating an evidence request To identify submissions and organize evidence
Uploaded files When a client submits evidence To deliver compliance evidence to the auditor
Notes and comments When a client explains missing items To communicate context to the auditor
Payment information When purchasing credits To process your payment (handled by Stripe)

Information Collected Automatically

We collect minimal technical information necessary to operate the Service:

  • IP addresses: Collected in server logs for security and debugging purposes. Not associated with your identity.
  • Timestamps: When requests are created, when files are uploaded, when submissions are made.
  • File metadata: File names, sizes, and types (not the contents of files).

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not use cookies for tracking purposes
  • We do not use analytics scripts (no Google Analytics, etc.)
  • We do not collect device fingerprints
  • We do not build user profiles
  • We do not track your browsing behavior

3 How We Use Your Data

We use your data only for the following purposes:

  • To provide the Service: Processing evidence requests, storing uploaded files, generating download links, and delivering evidence to auditors.
  • To communicate with you: Sending confirmation emails, submission receipts, and deadline reminders.
  • To process payments: Completing credit purchases through our payment processor, Stripe.
  • To maintain security: Detecting and preventing fraud, abuse, or security threats.
  • To improve the Service: Analyzing aggregate, anonymized usage patterns to improve functionality (we do not analyze individual user behavior).

Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

For users in the European Economic Area, we process your data under the following legal bases: (a) Contract performance — to provide the Service you've requested; (b) Legitimate interests — to maintain security and improve our Service; (c) Consent — where you've opted in to receive communications.

4 Data Sharing & Third Parties

We share your data only in the following limited circumstances:

With the Intended Recipient

When a client uploads evidence, that evidence (files, notes, and associated metadata) is shared with the auditor who requested it. This is the core function of the Service.

With Service Providers (Subprocessors)

We use the following third-party services to operate ProofRepo:

Provider Purpose Data Shared Location
Cloudflare Hosting, storage, CDN All uploaded files, metadata Global (US-based company)
Stripe Payment processing Email, payment card details USA
Resend Email delivery Email addresses, email content USA

These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only for the purposes we specify.

We Do NOT Share Data For

  • Advertising or marketing by third parties
  • Sale to data brokers
  • Any purpose other than providing the Service

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your data if required by law, such as in response to a valid court order, subpoena, or government request. We will notify you of such requests where legally permitted.

5 Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide the Service. Our retention periods are:

Data Type Retention Period Rationale
Uploaded evidence files 90 days Allows auditors time to download; auto-deleted after
Submission manifests 90 days Tied to evidence file retention
Evidence request metadata 30 days Necessary for request lifecycle
Credit/billing records 365 days Required for accounting and tax purposes
Email delivery logs 30 days For troubleshooting delivery issues

Automatic Deletion

All uploaded files are automatically and permanently deleted after 90 days. This is enforced at the infrastructure level — deletion is guaranteed and cannot be overridden.

You can also delete your submission immediately at any time by using the delete function in the Pack Viewer.

6 Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption at rest: All files are encrypted using AES-256 encryption.
  • Encryption in transit: All data transfers use TLS 1.2 or higher.
  • Access controls: File access requires cryptographically signed URLs that expire after 72 hours.
  • No accounts: Our link-based model eliminates password-related vulnerabilities.
  • SOC 2 infrastructure: Our infrastructure providers (Cloudflare, Stripe) maintain SOC 2 Type II certification.

For more details, see our Security Practices page.

7 Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten"): Request deletion of your data. Note: You can delete submissions immediately through the Pack Viewer, and all data is automatically deleted after 90 days.
  • Right to Data Portability: Request your data in a structured, machine-readable format (JSON).
  • Right to Object: Object to processing of your data in certain circumstances.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@proofrepo.com.

For California Residents (CCPA)

California residents have the right to know what personal information we collect, request deletion, and opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information.

For EU/EEA Residents (GDPR)

You have the rights listed above. If you believe we have violated your rights, you may lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

8 Cookies & Tracking

Minimal Cookie Use

ProofRepo does not use cookies for tracking, advertising, or analytics purposes. We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook pixels, or any third-party tracking scripts.

We may use essential, session-based cookies strictly necessary for the Service to function (e.g., to maintain form state during a multi-step process). These cookies:

  • Are not used to track you across websites
  • Are not shared with third parties
  • Expire when you close your browser or complete the process

9 Children's Privacy

ProofRepo is not intended for use by children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected data from a child, please contact us immediately at privacy@proofrepo.com, and we will delete it promptly.

We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Your continued use of the Service after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
