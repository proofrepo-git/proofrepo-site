Data Encryption

All evidence files and metadata uploaded to ProofRepo are protected by industry-standard encryption at every stage.

Encryption at Rest

All files are automatically encrypted using AES-256 (Advanced Encryption Standard with 256-bit keys) — the same encryption standard used by banks, governments, and Fortune 500 companies. Encryption is applied automatically by our infrastructure provider, Cloudflare, and requires no action from you.

Encryption in Transit

All data transmitted to and from ProofRepo is secured using TLS 1.2 or higher (Transport Layer Security). This encrypts your files during upload and download, protecting them from interception. We enforce HTTPS on all connections — plaintext HTTP is not supported.

Secure Infrastructure

ProofRepo is built entirely on enterprise-grade, SOC 2-certified cloud infrastructure. We don't operate our own data centers — instead, we leverage the security investments of industry-leading providers.

Why This Matters

By building on Cloudflare's infrastructure, your data benefits from the same security controls, physical security, and compliance certifications that protect some of the world's largest websites and applications. Cloudflare's global network spans 300+ cities and is designed to be resilient against DDoS attacks, data breaches, and infrastructure failures.

Access Controls

ProofRepo uses a secure, link-based access model that minimizes risk while maximizing convenience.

  • No accounts required: Clients don't need to create accounts or manage passwords. This eliminates credential-based attack vectors.
  • Cryptographically signed URLs: All download links are signed with HMAC-SHA256. Links cannot be forged, guessed, or tampered with.
  • Time-limited access: Download links expire after 72 hours. Even if a link is compromised, the window of exposure is limited.
  • Unique tokens per request: Each evidence request generates a unique, random token. Tokens cannot be enumerated or predicted.
  • No persistent sessions: We don't store cookies or maintain persistent sessions that could be hijacked.

Data Retention & Deletion

We believe in data minimization — keeping your data only as long as necessary and giving you full control over deletion.

90-Day Automatic Deletion

All uploaded files and manifests are automatically and permanently deleted 90 days after upload. This is enforced at the infrastructure level through Cloudflare R2 lifecycle policies — deletion is guaranteed and irreversible.

On-Demand Deletion

Don't want to wait 90 days? Anyone with access to a submission's Pack Viewer can permanently delete all files immediately. Simply type "DELETE" to confirm, and all associated files are removed within seconds. This action cannot be undone.

What We Don't Do

Security isn't just about what we do — it's also about what we deliberately don't do.

  • We don't access your files: Your evidence files are encrypted and we have no business reason to access them. ProofRepo employees do not view customer files.
  • We don't sell your data: Your data is never sold, rented, or shared with third parties for marketing or any other purpose.
  • We don't use tracking scripts: No Google Analytics, Facebook pixels, or third-party trackers. We don't build profiles on our users.
  • We don't store passwords: Our link-based access model means there are no passwords to steal or leak.
  • We don't retain data indefinitely: Unlike many services that keep data forever "just in case," we automatically delete everything after 90 days.

Incident Response

In the unlikely event of a security incident affecting your data, we commit to:

  • Prompt notification: We will notify affected users within 72 hours of discovering a breach that may have exposed their data.
  • Transparent communication: We will clearly explain what happened, what data may have been affected, and what steps we're taking to address it.
  • Remediation: We will take immediate steps to contain the incident, assess the damage, and prevent recurrence.
